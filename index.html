Summary

Fast Growers are companies that grow at about 20 – 25% per year. Also, they have PEG ratio's of 1 or less.

Stalwarts are companies that grow at about 10 – 12% per year. They have multibillion dollar market caps and may include a dividend.

Slow Growers are companies that grow at about 2 – 4% per year. Also, they include a dividened.

Turnarounds are in, near or just coming out bankruptcy.

Asset Plays are companies that have some kind of asset like cash, real estate or shares in another company.

Cyclicals are companies that grow and contract routinely.
